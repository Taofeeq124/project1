Our Management

Our Management

Elijah Onyeagba - Managing Director/CEO

Elijah is an Economist by training and has an MBA with over a decade experience in commercial, corporate, retail and mortgage banking. He has structured construction finance deals worth over US$150m(NGN30bn) and over US$100m(NGN20bn) in retail, commercial and investment mortgages.

Before now, He was the Group Head in charge of Real Estate Transaction in ASO Savings & Loans Plc; Nigeria’s largest originator of mortgage assets with additional responsibility of overseeing the company business concerns within the Federal Capital Territory, North and the Southern Region of Nigeria. Prior to joining ASO, he worked with Equitorial Trust Bank (Now Sterling Bank), Bond Bank (Now Skye Bank) and Platinum Bank (Now keystone Bank).

An Archbishop Desmond Tutu Fellow of the African Leadership Institute, He is also a Senior Member of the Chartered institute of Bankers of Nigeria (HCIB) and a Fellow of the Institute of Treasury and Financial Administration (FITFA), a new leader with the Crans Montana Forum.

He was a consultant to the World Bank on Housing Finance, Land Reforms and Urban Renewal, and Doing Business in Nigeria (DBN), and was the coordinator for the Nigeria Vision 20:2020 National Technical Working Group on Housing, and later selected to serve on the 27 man Central Working Committee that produced the final blue print for Nigeria.

Elijah has undergone lots of leadership and management courses in Harvard, Wharton, Oxford (SBS) and INSEAD business schools. Elijah speaks at conferences and seminars nationally and internationally. Elijah has won numerous awards for his contribution to youth empowerment, education and community development.

Margaret Ekeoba - Head, Operations

Margaret has an MBA in Human Resources with close to a decade work experience in office administration, banking operations, strategy, retail and mortgage banking. She was responsible for organizing the 1st edition of the ASO Savings and Loans Plc. Affordable Housing Conference which attracted participants from across the globe to Abuja, Nigeria.

Why Choose Us?

We conduct ourselves and our business affairs in accordance with the highest ethical standards and in compliance with all applicable laws, striving always to be a respected corporate citizen worldwide.

Integrity

This is central to the way we do business and crucial to our external credibility and continued respect in the market place. To demonstrate integrity everyone must follow ethical business practices, be reliable, trustworthy and honest, deliver our promises and have a fair minded approach.

Innovation

CHC Limited is challenging conventions and realising innovation in design, development and delivery. We will continually improve, embrace change and provide employees with the opportunity to learn and develop, creating an environment where people are encouraged to demonstrate innovation by successfully implementing new ideas.

Resilience

Being able to adapt quickly to the volatile, ever-changing global environment is crucial to a company's survival. Our employees must be agile enough to react swiftly to new trends and developments and change the way we do things. Only by keeping up with the times and change can CHC achieve sustainable success.
